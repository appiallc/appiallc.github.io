Engagement Model

Our engagement model outlines how our consultancy will work with clients to deliver services. Varied based on your business model, services offered, and client needs, below is a general framework for our engagement model:

Consultation

Understand client requirements, challenges, and goals. Define project scope and desired results.

Solution Development and Launch

Develop a tailored IT solution and project plan. Conduct a kickoff meeting to introduce the team, set communication channels, and define milestones, KPIs, and success criteria.

Build, Validate, and Deploy

Implement the plan, including software development, system integration, and IT infrastructure setup. Provide regular updates, ensure quality, deploy solutions, and integrate new systems.

Documentation, Training, and Support

Complete project documentation and provide a training manual. Offer post-implementation support, monitor system performance, apply updates, and resolve issues promptly.

Client Engagement and Continuous Improvement

Maintain client relationship, offer additional support, seek collaboration opportunities, gather feedback for improvement, refine engagement model.
