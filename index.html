OUR APPROACH

Engagement Model

Our engagement model outlines how our consultancy will work with clients to deliver services. Varied based on your business model, services offered, and client needs, below is a general framework for our engagement model:

STEP 1

Initial Consultation

Identify the client's needs, challenges, and objectives. Determine the scope of work and the desired outcomes.

STEP 2

Proposal and Contract

Present a detailed proposal outlining services, timelines, costs, and deliverables. Negotiate terms and sign a contract with the client.

STEP 3

Project Kickoff

Hold a kickoff meeting to introduce the team and establish communication channels. Define project milestones, key performance indicators (KPIs), and success criteria.

STEP 4

Assessment and Analysis

Conduct a thorough analysis of the client's current IT infrastructure, processes, and systems. Identify areas for improvement and create a roadmap for the engagement.

STEP 5

Solution Design

Develop a tailored IT solution that addresses the client's specific needs and challenges. Create a detailed project plan, including technology recommendations and architecture.

STEP 6

Implementation and Development

Execute the plan, which may include software development, system integration, and IT infrastructure setup. Regularly update the client on progress and address any issues or changes.

STEP 7

Quality Assurance and Testing

Ensure that all solutions and systems meet quality standards through rigorous testing. Resolve any defects or issues that arise during testing.

STEP 8

Deployment and Integration

Deploy the IT solutions into the client's environment. Integrate new systems with existing ones, ensuring seamless operation.

STEP 9

Documentation, Training and Knowledge Transfer

Ensure all project documentation, including user manuals and technical documentation, is complete. Provide training to the client's team to use and maintain the implemented solutions. Transfer knowledge and documentation for ongoing support.

STEP 10

Ongoing Support and Maintenance

Offer post-implementation support and maintenance services as per the agreed-upon terms. Monitor system performance, apply updates, and resolve issues promptly.

STEP 11

Client Relationship Management

Maintain an ongoing relationship with the client, offering additional services and support as needed. Seek opportunities for future collaborations and referrals.

STEP 12

Feedback and Continuous Improvement

Collect feedback from both your team and the client to identify areas for improvement in your consultancy's processes and services. Continuously refine your engagement model based on lessons learned.
